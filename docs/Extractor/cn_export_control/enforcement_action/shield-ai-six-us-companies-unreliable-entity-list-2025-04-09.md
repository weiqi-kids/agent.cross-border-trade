## Summary

China's Unreliable Entity List Working Mechanism (不可靠实体清单工作机制) issued Announcement No.8 of 2025, adding six US entities — including Shield AI, Inc. — to the Unreliable Entity List. The listed entities are prohibited from China-related import/export activities and from making new investments within China, with the measures effective from 12:01 on April 10, 2025.

## Key Details

- **Issuing Authority**: Unreliable Entity List Working Mechanism (不可靠实体清单工作机制), with MOFCOM affixing the seal (商务部代章)
- **Effective Date**: 2025-04-10, 12:01 (2025年4月10日12时01分)
- **Scope**: Six US defense, aerospace, autonomy and technology companies; restricts their China-related trade and investment

## Key Provisions

- **Legal basis**: 《对外贸易法》(Foreign Trade Law), 《国家安全法》(National Security Law), 《反外国制裁法》(Anti-Foreign Sanctions Law); and 《不可靠实体清单规定》(Provisions on the Unreliable Entity List) Articles 2, 8 and 10.
- **Six listed US entities**:
  1. Shield AI, Inc. (护盾人工智能公司)
  2. Sierra Nevada Corporation (内华达山脉公司)
  3. Cyberlux Corporation (赛博勒克斯公司)
  4. Edge Autonomy Operations LLC (边缘自治运营公司)
  5. Group W (Group W公司)
  6. Hudson Technologies Co. (哈德森技术公司)
- **Measures imposed** (two):
  - Prohibition on engaging in China-related import and export activities (禁止其从事与中国有关的进出口活动).
  - Prohibition on making new investments within China (禁止其在中国境内新增投资).

## Supply Chain Impact Assessment

*The following is speculative analysis.* Placement on the Unreliable Entity List severs the six US firms' ability to trade with or invest in China, affecting defense-tech, autonomous systems and aerospace companies with potential China-facing operations or sourcing. The measures, taken amid the April 2025 US-China tariff escalation, signal an integrated countermeasure posture combining the UEL, export controls and tariffs. Counterparties should screen against the UEL to avoid secondary compliance exposure.
