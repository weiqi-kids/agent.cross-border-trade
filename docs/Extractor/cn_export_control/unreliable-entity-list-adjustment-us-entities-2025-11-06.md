## Summary

MOFCOM announced adjustments to Unreliable Entity List measures for US entities following the China-US Kuala Lumpur economic and trade consultation. Starting November 10, 2025, China will continue to suspend measures from the April 4 announcement for one year, and stop measures from the March 4 announcements. Domestic companies may apply to conduct transactions with listed entities, subject to review by the Unreliable Entity List Working Mechanism.

## Key Details

- **Issuing Authority**: Ministry of Commerce of PRC (MOFCOM) / Unreliable Entity List Working Mechanism
- **Effective Date**: 2025-11-10
- **Legal Basis**: Anti-Foreign Sanctions Law, Unreliable Entity List Provisions

## Key Provisions

### Background
- March 4, 2025 (Announcements No. 5 and 6): US entities added to Unreliable Entity List
- April 4, 2025 (Announcement No. 7): Additional US entities added to Unreliable Entity List

### Adjustment Measures (Effective November 10, 2025)
1. **April 4 announcement entities**: Measures **continued suspension for 1 year**
2. **March 4 announcement entities**: Measures **stopped**

### Transaction Application Process
- Domestic companies may apply to conduct transactions with listed entities
- Applications reviewed by the Unreliable Entity List Working Mechanism
- Compliant applications will be approved
