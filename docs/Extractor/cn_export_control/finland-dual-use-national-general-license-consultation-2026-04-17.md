## Summary

Finland's Ministry for Foreign Affairs has launched a public consultation on two draft decisions for national general licenses covering the export of dual-use items. The consultation deadline is April 22, 2026. Finland plans to issue two new national general export licenses: one targeting public institutions and one targeting companies exporting items to South Korea. Public responses can be submitted via the lausuntopalvelu.fi platform or by email to the Ministry.

## Key Details

- **Issuing Authority**: Finland Ministry for Foreign Affairs
- **Effective Date**: Consultation deadline April 22, 2026; final license issuance date TBD
- **Scope**: Dual-use items; national general export licenses for public institutions and exports to South Korea
