# 印度扩大跨国公司内部转让全球授权(GAICT)所涵盖的SCOMET物项范围（India Expands SCOMET Items Covered by Global Authorization for Intra-Company Transfer）

## Summary
India's Directorate General of Foreign Trade issued Notification No. 26/2024-2025 on October 7, 2024, revising Appendix 10M of the Handbook of Procedures. The modification expands the range of dual-use items, software, and technology in the SCOMET list covered by the Global Authorization for Intra-Company Transfer (GAICT) program for multinational corporations.

## Key Details
- **Issuing Authority**: India Directorate General of Foreign Trade (DGFT)
- **Effective Date**: 2024-10-07
- **Scope**: SCOMET (Special Chemicals, Organisms, Materials, Equipment, and Technologies) items eligible for GAICT streamlined procedures

## Key Provisions
- **Notification Number**: 26/2024-2025
- **Legal Framework**: Revises Appendix 10M of Handbook of Procedures (HBP) under 2023 Foreign Trade Policy
- **Expanded Coverage**: Increases the range of dual-use items, software, and technology in SCOMET list eligible for GAICT
- **Program Objective**: Streamlines export and re-export procedures for SCOMET items transferred within multinational company operations
- **Geographic Scope**: Applies to transfers to/from countries listed in HBP Section 10.15, Table 1
- **Intra-Company Focus**: Facilitates internal technology and equipment transfers within same corporate structure
