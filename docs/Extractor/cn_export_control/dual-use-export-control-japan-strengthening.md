# 商务部新闻发言人就加强两用物项对日本出口管制答记者问 (MOFCOM Spokesperson Answers Questions on Strengthening Dual-Use Export Controls to Japan)

**Source:** 商务部新闻办公室
**URL:** http://exportcontrol.mofcom.gov.cn/article/gndt/202601/1204.html
**Date:** 2026-01-06
**Category:** enforcement_action
**Fetched:** 2026-01-28
**Confidence:** High

---

## Summary

商务部宣布加强对日本的两用物项出口管制,禁止向日本军事用户、军事用途及一切有助于提升日本军事实力的最终用户出口所有两用物项。此措施因日本领导人就台湾问题发表涉及武力干预的言论而采取,违反一个中国原则。任何国家和地区组织或个人违规转移原产于中国的两用物项至日本将面临法律追究。

---

## Key Details

**Issuing Authority:**
- 商务部 (Ministry of Commerce)

**Effective Date:** 2026-01-06 (宣布日期,具体生效日期未明确指出)

**Scope:**
- 所有两用物项 (All dual-use items)
- 针对日本军事用户 (Japanese military end-users)
- 针对日本军事用途 (Military use in Japan)
- 针对一切有助于提升日本军事实力的其他最终用户用途 (All other end-user purposes that enhance Japan's military capabilities)

---

## Key Provisions

1. **Export Prohibition:** 禁止向"日本军事用户、军事用途,以及一切有助于提升日本军事实力的其他最终用户用途"出口所有两用物项 (Prohibit export of all dual-use items to "Japanese military end-users, military purposes, and all other end-user purposes that enhance Japan's military capabilities")

2. **Legal Basis:** 根据《中华人民共和国出口管制法》和《中华人民共和国两用物项出口管制条例》实施 (Implemented in accordance with Export Control Law of the PRC and Regulations on Export Control of Dual-Use Items of the PRC)

3. **Third-Party Transfer Prohibition:** 任何国家和地区的组织或个人若"违反上述规定,将原产于中华人民共和国的相关两用物项转移或提供给日本"将面临法律追究 (Any organization or individual from any country or region that "violates the above provisions and transfers or provides relevant dual-use items originating from the PRC to Japan" will face legal consequences)

4. **Policy Trigger:** 日本领导人就台湾问题发表言论,"暗示武力介入台海可能性",被认为违背一个中国原则 (Japanese leadership made statements on Taiwan issue "suggesting possibility of military intervention in Taiwan Strait", considered to violate One-China Principle)

---

## Supply Chain Impact Assessment

**Japan-China Trade Disruption:** 该措施可能显著影响日中双边贸易中涉及两用物项的供应链,日本制造业尤其是半导体、精密仪器、航空航天等领域可能面临原材料和零部件供应中断风险 (This measure may significantly impact Japan-China bilateral trade supply chains involving dual-use items; Japanese manufacturing, especially semiconductors, precision instruments, and aerospace sectors may face risks of raw material and component supply disruption) *(speculative)*

**End-User Verification Challenges:** 企业需建立更严格的最终用户审查机制以确定出口是否"有助于提升日本军事实力",这一宽泛定义可能导致审批标准不确定性增加 (Enterprises need to establish stricter end-user verification mechanisms to determine if exports "enhance Japan's military capabilities"; this broad definition may lead to increased uncertainty in approval standards) *(speculative)*

**Re-Export Control:** 第三方转移禁令延伸了管辖范围,其他国家和地区的企业在涉及中国原产两用物项转售至日本时需谨慎评估法律风险 (Third-party transfer prohibition extends jurisdictional scope; enterprises in other countries and regions need to carefully assess legal risks when reselling China-origin dual-use items to Japan) *(speculative)*

**Geopolitical Trade Impact:** 该措施体现了中国将出口管制作为外交政策工具的意愿,未来类似措施可能扩展至其他地区 (This measure reflects China's willingness to use export controls as a foreign policy tool; similar measures may extend to other regions in the future) *(speculative)*

---

## Notes

此声明是中国首次针对特定国家(日本)实施全面的两用物项出口禁令,且明确将政治因素(台湾问题)作为管制触发条件。"一切有助于提升日本军事实力的其他最终用户用途"的措辞具有高度不确定性,实际执行中可能涵盖广泛的民用最终用户。企业应密切关注后续实施细则和案例,建立完善的出口合规审查程序。该措施的法律依据援引《出口管制法》和《两用物项出口管制条例》,但未说明具体的法律条款或程序,执行细节有待明确。
