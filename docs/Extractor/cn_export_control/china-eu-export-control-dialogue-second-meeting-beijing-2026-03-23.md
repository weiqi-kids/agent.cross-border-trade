## Summary

On March 23, 2026, the second meeting of the upgraded China-EU Export Control Dialogue Mechanism was held in Beijing. China's MOFCOM Bureau of Security and Control Director General Jiang Qianliang met with European Commission DG Trade Deputy Director General Ledonne to conduct in-depth and constructive communication on each side's respective concerns in the field of export controls. Both parties agreed to continue maintaining communication and to promote the stability and smooth functioning of China-EU industrial and supply chains.
