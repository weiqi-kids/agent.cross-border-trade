# 关于优化调整石墨物项临时出口管制措施的公告 (Announcement on Optimizing and Adjusting Interim Export Control Measures for Graphite Items)

**Source:** 商务部、海关总署
**URL:** http://exportcontrol.mofcom.gov.cn/article/zcfg/gnzcfg/zcfggzqd/202310/912.html
**Date:** 2023-10-20
**Category:** controlled_item_change
**Fetched:** 2026-01-28
**Confidence:** High

---

## Summary

商务部、海关总署发布公告,优化调整石墨物项的临时出口管制措施。自2023年12月1日起,对高纯度人造石墨材料及天然鳞片石墨(含球化石墨、膨胀石墨)实施出口许可证管理,未经许可不得出口。该公告废止2006年第50号公告中的相关规定。

---

## Key Details

**Issuing Authority:**
- 商务部 (Ministry of Commerce)
- 海关总署 (General Administration of Customs)

**Effective Date:** 2023-12-01

**Scope:**
- 人造石墨材料及其制品 (Artificial graphite materials and products) - 需同时满足纯度>99.9%、抗折强度>30MPa、密度>1.73g/cm³
- 天然鳞片石墨及其制品 (Natural flake graphite and products) - 包括球化石墨 (spheroidized graphite)、膨胀石墨 (expanded graphite)

---

## Key Provisions

1. **Export License Requirement:** 出口经营者须向商务部申请许可证,提交出口合同、技术说明、最终用户证明、进口商情况介绍和身份证明等文件 (Exporters must apply to MOFCOM for license, submitting export contract, technical specifications, end-user certificate, importer information and identification)

2. **Review Process:** 商务部在法定期限内审查,对国家安全有重大影响的物项出口由商务部会同有关部门报国务院批准 (MOFCOM reviews within statutory timeframe; exports with significant national security impact require State Council approval after joint review with relevant departments)

3. **Legal Consequences:** 违规出口将受行政处罚,构成犯罪依法追究刑事责任 (Violations face administrative penalties; criminal liability pursued where crimes are constituted)

4. **Superseded Regulation:** 废止《商务部 海关总署公告2006年第50号》中的其他石墨物项管制措施 (Repeals other graphite item control measures in MOFCOM-GAC Announcement No. 50 of 2006)

5. **Technical Thresholds for Artificial Graphite:**
   - 纯度 (Purity): >99.9%
   - 抗折强度 (Flexural strength): >30MPa
   - 密度 (Density): >1.73g/cm³

---

## Supply Chain Impact Assessment

**Battery Industry Impact:** 球化石墨是锂离子电池负极材料的核心组分,该管制措施可能影响全球电动汽车电池供应链,中国占全球球化石墨产能约70%以上 (Spheroidized graphite is a core component of lithium-ion battery anode materials; this control measure may impact global EV battery supply chains, with China accounting for over 70% of global spheroidized graphite capacity) *(speculative)*

**Steel Industry Consideration:** 膨胀石墨用于耐火材料和密封材料,冶金行业可能面临采购流程延长 (Expanded graphite is used in refractory and sealing materials; metallurgical industry may face extended procurement processes) *(speculative)*

**Strategic Material Control:** 石墨管制强化了中国对新能源产业链关键原材料的出口管理,预期将提升出口许可审批的严格程度 (Graphite controls strengthen China's export management of key raw materials in new energy industry chains, expected to increase stringency of export license approvals) *(speculative)*

**Compliance Burden:** 出口企业需准备详细的技术文件和最终用户证明,合规成本和时间将显著增加 (Exporting enterprises need to prepare detailed technical documentation and end-user certificates; compliance costs and time will significantly increase) *(speculative)*

---

## Notes

此公告是中国首次对石墨物项实施系统性出口管制,背景是全球新能源汽车和储能产业快速发展背景下的战略资源保护。技术参数门槛的设定表明管制针对高性能石墨材料,而非普通工业石墨。企业应特别注意球化石墨和膨胀石墨的明确纳入,这两类产品在新能源和高温工业领域应用广泛。建议相关出口企业提前与主管部门沟通,了解审批要求和时限。
