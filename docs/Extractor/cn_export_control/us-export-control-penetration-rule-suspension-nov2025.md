# 商务部新闻发言人就美方暂停实施出口管制穿透性规则答记者问 (MOFCOM Spokesperson Responds to US Suspension of Export Control Penetration Rule)

**Source:** 商务部新闻办公室
**URL:** http://exportcontrol.mofcom.gov.cn/article/gndt/202511/1199.html
**Date:** 2025-11-12
**Category:** international_cooperation
**Fetched:** 2026-01-28
**Confidence:** High

---

## Summary

商务部新闻发言人对美国商务部产业与安全局(BIS)宣布暂停实施出口管制穿透性规则一年(2025年11月10日至2026年11月9日)作出回应。在此期间,美国出口管制"实体清单"中的企业及其超过50%持股的关联企业将不受穿透性规则追加制裁。中方称此举是"美方落实中美吉隆坡经贸磋商共识的重要举措",并表示双方将继续就一年后的安排进行讨论。

---

## Key Details

**Issuing Authority:**
- 商务部新闻办公室 (MOFCOM Press Office)

**Effective Date:** 2025-11-10 to 2026-11-09 (US suspension period)

**Scope:**
- 美国出口管制"实体清单" (US Export Control Entity List)
- 穿透性规则 (Penetration Rule / Foreign Direct Product Rule)
- 实体清单企业及其超过50%持股的关联企业 (Entity List companies and their affiliates with >50% ownership)

---

## Key Provisions

1. **US Suspension Announcement:** 美国商务部产业与安全局宣布"于2025年11月10日至2026年11月9日暂停实施出口管制穿透性规则" (US Department of Commerce Bureau of Industry and Security announces "suspension of export control penetration rule from November 10, 2025 to November 9, 2026")

2. **Scope of Relief:** 在暂停期间,美国出口管制"实体清单"中的企业及其超过50%持股的关联企业将不受穿透性规则追加制裁 (During suspension period, Entity List companies and their affiliates with >50% ownership will not be subject to additional sanctions under penetration rule)

3. **China's Assessment:** 中方称此举是"美方落实中美吉隆坡经贸磋商共识的重要举措" (China describes this as "important measure by US to implement consensus reached in China-US Kuala Lumpur economic and trade consultations")

4. **Future Negotiations:** 双方将继续就一年后的安排进行讨论 (Both sides will continue discussions on arrangements after one year)

5. **China's Policy Stance:** 中方表示愿与美方"秉持相互尊重、平等协商的原则,加强对话交流",以促进两国企业合作和全球产业链稳定 (China expresses willingness to "uphold principles of mutual respect and equal consultation, strengthen dialogue and exchange" to promote cooperation between enterprises of both countries and global supply chain stability)

---

## Supply Chain Impact Assessment

**Temporary Relief for Chinese Firms:** 穿透性规则的暂停为实体清单上的中国企业及其子公司提供一年的喘息期,预期将缓解这些企业在获取美国技术和零部件方面的压力 (Suspension of penetration rule provides one-year reprieve for Chinese companies on Entity List and their subsidiaries, expected to ease pressure on these firms in accessing US technology and components) *(speculative)*

**Supply Chain Flexibility:** 超过50%持股的关联企业免于追加制裁,可能恢复部分正常商业交易,有助于稳定半导体、电信设备等关键行业的供应链 (Affiliates with >50% ownership exempt from additional sanctions may restore some normal commercial transactions, helping stabilize supply chains in key industries like semiconductors and telecom equipment) *(speculative)*

**Policy Uncertainty After One Year:** 暂停期限仅为一年,企业面临2026年11月后政策可能恢复的不确定性,可能影响长期投资和供应链布局决策 (Suspension limited to one year; enterprises face uncertainty about possible policy resumption after November 2026, may impact long-term investment and supply chain configuration decisions) *(speculative)*

**China-US Trade Relations Indicator:** 此举被中方视为"重要举措",暗示中美经贸关系可能进入阶段性缓和期,但需观察后续谈判进展 (This move viewed by China as "important measure", suggests China-US economic relations may enter phased détente period, but need to observe subsequent negotiation progress) *(speculative)*

---

## Notes

穿透性规则(Penetration Rule)也称"外国直接产品规则"(Foreign Direct Product Rule, FDPR),是美国出口管制的域外管辖工具,规定即使产品非美国制造,只要使用了美国技术或软件,仍需遵守美国出口管制规定。该规则在华为等中国企业受制裁后被美国广泛应用,对全球半导体供应链产生深远影响。美方此次暂停实施被视为中美吉隆坡经贸磋商的成果之一,但暂停期限仅为一年,显示美方保留了政策工具的灵活性。中方对此表示欢迎,但强调需继续对话协商一年后的安排,暗示双方对长期政策走向仍需进一步谈判。对于实体清单上的中国企业而言,这一年的窗口期是重要的供应链调整和技术自主化的缓冲时间。
