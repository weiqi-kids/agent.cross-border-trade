# 对有关物项实施出口管制 (Export Control on Aerospace Materials and Equipment)

## Summary

Three government departments issued Announcement No. 21 of 2024, implementing export controls on aerospace manufacturing equipment and ultra-high molecular weight polyethylene fiber, effective July 1, 2024. The announcement establishes controls on four categories: aerospace superplastic forming equipment, gas turbine engine directional crystal casting equipment, spacesuit visor manufacturing equipment, and high-performance UHMWPE fiber.

---

## Key Details

**Issuing Authority:**
- Ministry of Commerce (商务部)
- General Administration of Customs (海关总署)
- Central Military Commission Equipment Development Department (中央军委装备发展部)

**Announcement Number:** 2024 No. 21

**Effective Date:** 2024-07-01

---

## Key Provisions

### 1. Aerospace Manufacturing Equipment and Software

**Superplastic Forming Tools (超塑成形工具和模具):**
- Tools and dies for titanium/aluminum alloy superplastic forming
- Application: Aircraft and spacecraft component production
- Process: High-temperature forming of complex aerospace structures

### 2. Gas Turbine Engine Equipment

**Directional Crystal Casting Equipment (定向凝固设备):**
- Equipment for producing turbine blades with directional crystal structure
- Application: High-temperature sections of jet engines and gas turbines
- Includes: Precision casting intermediate products

**Technical Significance (speculative):**
- Directional crystal casting critical for advanced turbine blade performance
- Technology enhances high-temperature strength and creep resistance
- Key capability for military jet engines and commercial aviation powerplants

### 3. Spacesuit Visor Manufacturing Equipment

**Specialized Molds (专用模具):**
- "Specially designed for manufacturing aerospace suit face windows"
- Application: Production of transparent visors for space suits
- Technical requirements: Optical quality, pressure resistance, thermal protection

### 4. Ultra-High Molecular Weight Polyethylene (UHMWPE) Fiber

**Technical Specifications:**
- Tensile strength: ≥40 cN/dtex
- Initial modulus: ≥1600 cN/dtex

**Applications (speculative):**
- Ballistic protection (body armor, vehicle armor)
- Aerospace composites (lightweight structural components)
- Marine applications (high-strength ropes, tethers)
- Medical implants (joint replacement components)

### 5. Export Licensing Procedures

**Application Process:**
- Submit through provincial commerce departments to Ministry of Commerce
- Required documentation:
  - Export contracts
  - Technical specifications
  - End-user certificates
  - Identity documentation

**Enforcement:**
"违反本公告规定的,依据有关法律法规承担相应的行政责任或刑事责任" — Unauthorized export or violations result in administrative penalties or criminal liability per applicable regulations.

## Supply Chain Impact Assessment

**Impact Level:** High

**Affected Industries:**

**Aerospace Manufacturing:**
- Aircraft structural component manufacturers
- Jet engine and gas turbine producers
- Spacecraft systems manufacturers
- Space suit and protective equipment producers

**Advanced Materials:**
- UHMWPE fiber manufacturers
- Composite materials producers
- Ballistic protection equipment manufacturers
- High-performance rope and textile producers

**Geographic Considerations (speculative):**
- China has developed significant aerospace manufacturing capabilities, competing with Western suppliers
- Export controls may target technology transfer to emerging aerospace industries
- UHMWPE fiber controls impact global supply chains for protective equipment
- Western aerospace companies may already limit reliance on Chinese suppliers for critical manufacturing equipment

**Technology Transfer Implications (speculative):**
- Controls on manufacturing equipment and software limit technology diffusion
- Directional crystal casting technology particularly sensitive for military applications
- UHMWPE fiber dual-use nature (civilian and military applications) requires end-user scrutiny
- Spacesuit visor equipment controls may impact commercial space industry development

**Compliance Requirements:**
- Manufacturers must classify products against technical specifications
- Aerospace equipment requires detailed end-user verification
- UHMWPE fiber requires tensile strength and modulus testing documentation
- Technology transfer agreements may require separate licensing

## Notes

This announcement targets specialized aerospace manufacturing technologies rather than finished aerospace products. The inclusion of the Central Military Commission Equipment Development Department as a joint issuing authority emphasizes the military significance of these technologies.

The UHMWPE fiber specifications target high-performance applications, particularly ballistic protection. The technical thresholds (40 cN/dtex tensile strength, 1600 cN/dtex modulus) indicate advanced materials suitable for military body armor and aerospace composites.

The specificity of controlled items—superplastic forming tools, directional crystal casting equipment, spacesuit visor molds—suggests these represent capabilities where China has developed competitive advantages and seeks to manage technology diffusion.
