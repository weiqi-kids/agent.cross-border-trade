## Summary
This notice provides comprehensive guidance to enterprises and individuals on preventing diversion of precursor chemicals and drug manufacturing equipment, emphasizing criminal prohibitions, tax compliance, international trade restrictions, and reporting obligations. The notice specifically identifies high-risk export destinations including the United States, Canada, and Mexico.

## Key Details
- **Issuing Authority**: National Narcotics Control Commission Office (国家禁毒委员会办公室)
- **Effective Date**: 2025-11-10 (announcement date)
- **Scope**: Manufacturers, traders, shippers, logistics enterprises, and individuals involved in precursor chemical and equipment supply chains

## Key Provisions
- **Criminal Prohibitions**: Illegal production, sale, and transport of drug precursors including acetic anhydride and ether subject to criminal penalties
- **Tax Compliance**: Enterprises producing non-listed precursor chemicals must maintain proper tax documentation
- **Customs Enforcement**: Enhanced penalties for smuggling precursor chemicals
- **Internet Restrictions**: Prohibits online publication of sales information for precursor chemicals
- **International Trade Guidance**:
  - Emphasizes compliance with importing country laws
  - Identifies "high-risk countries" including the United States, Canada, and Mexico
  - Warns of potential legal consequences in destination countries
- **Reporting Obligations**: Postal, courier, and logistics enterprises must report suspicious activities
- **Legal Protections**: Chinese citizens may seek assistance from Chinese embassies if foreign enforcement actions are believed to exceed proper authority
- **Non-Listed Chemicals**: Extends vigilance to chemicals not explicitly listed but potentially usable in drug manufacturing
