## Summary
The UN Security Council unanimously adopted Resolution 2758(2024) on November 13, 2024, extending sanctions measures against Yemen for 12 months through November 15, 2025. The resolution extends travel bans and asset freezes while prolonging the expert panel mandate through December 15, 2025.

## Key Details
- **Issuing Authority**: United Nations Security Council
- **Effective Date**: 2024-11-13 (Resolution adoption); extends sanctions through 2025-11-15
- **Scope**: Travel bans, asset freezes, and expert panel monitoring for Yemen-related sanctions

## Key Provisions
- **Resolution Number**: 2758(2024)
- **Voting**: Unanimously adopted by Security Council
- **Sanctions Extension**: Travel bans and asset freezes extended for 12 months through November 15, 2025
- **Expert Panel**: Mandate of the Yemen Sanctions Committee expert panel extended 13 months through December 15, 2025
- **Monitoring Function**: Expert panel assists the Security Council's Yemen Sanctions Committee in implementation and compliance monitoring
