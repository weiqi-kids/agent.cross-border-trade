# 关于发布2024年度《两用物项和技术进出口许可证管理目录》的公告 (Announcement on Releasing the 2024 Dual-Use Items and Technology Import-Export License Management Catalog)

**Date:** 2023-12-29
**Category:** regulation_update
**Fetched:** 2026-01-28
**Confidence:** High

---

## Summary

商务部与海关总署联合发布《商务部 海关总署公告2023年第66号》,公布2024年度《两用物项和技术进出口许可证管理目录》。该目录根据《中华人民共和国出口管制法》进行调整,自2024年1月1日起施行,取代2022年第42号公告目录。

---

## Key Details

**Issuing Authority:**
- 中华人民共和国商务部 (Ministry of Commerce of the People's Republic of China)
- 中华人民共和国海关总署 (General Administration of Customs of the People's Republic of China)

**Effective Date:** 2024-01-01

**Scope:**
- 两用物项 (Dual-use items)
- 两用技术 (Dual-use technologies)
- 放射性同位素 (Radioactive isotopes)

---

## Key Provisions

1. **Legal Basis:** 根据《中华人民共和国出口管制法》《中华人民共和国对外贸易法》《中华人民共和国海关法》《中华人民共和国监控化学品管理条例》及《两用物项和技术进出口许可证管理办法》制定 (Formulated in accordance with Export Control Law, Foreign Trade Law, Customs Law, Controlled Chemicals Regulations, and Dual-Use License Management Measures)

2. **Radioactive Isotope Import Requirement:** 进口放射性同位素的单位,在申请办理两用物项进口许可证前,应按照有关规定取得生态环境部门颁发的"辐射安全许可证" (Units importing radioactive isotopes must obtain "Radiation Safety License" from ecological and environmental departments before applying for dual-use import license)

3. **Superseded Regulation:** 《商务部 海关总署公告2022年第42号》所附《两用物项和技术进出口许可证管理目录》同时废止 (The Dual-Use Catalog attached to Announcement No. 42 of 2022 is simultaneously repealed)

4. **Attachment:** 公告附有完整的2024年度管理目录PDF文件 (Announcement includes complete 2024 Management Catalog in PDF format)

5. **Administrative Contact:** 联系邮箱 zhouxiaohui@mofcom.gov.cn,技术支持电话 010-67801943 (Contact email: zhouxiaohui@mofcom.gov.cn, Technical support: 010-67801943)

## Supply Chain Impact Assessment

**Comprehensive Catalog Update:** 目录的年度调整可能影响广泛的两用物项贸易企业,企业需及时核对新目录以确保合规 (Annual catalog updates may affect a wide range of dual-use item trading enterprises; companies need to promptly review the new catalog to ensure compliance) *(speculative)*

**Radioactive Material Trade:** 放射性同位素进口增设前置审批要求,医疗、科研、工业用放射源供应链可能面临审批环节增加 (Additional pre-approval requirements for radioactive isotope imports may result in increased approval procedures for medical, research, and industrial radioactive source supply chains) *(speculative)*

**Regulatory Continuity:** 新旧目录衔接明确,避免了监管空白期,有利于维护贸易秩序稳定 (Clear transition between old and new catalogs avoids regulatory gaps, conducive to maintaining stable trade order) *(speculative)*

## Notes

此公告是中国两用物项出口管制年度例行更新的重要文件。企业应下载并仔细研读附件中的完整目录,对比2022年版本识别新增、删除或修订的管制物项。放射性同位素的前置许可要求体现了核安全监管的强化。建议相关企业建立目录监控机制,及时跟踪政策变化。
