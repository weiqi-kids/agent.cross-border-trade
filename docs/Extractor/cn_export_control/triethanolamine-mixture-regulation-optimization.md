# 优化低浓度三乙醇胺混合物进出口监管措施的通知 (Notice on Optimizing Import-Export Supervision of Low-Concentration Triethanolamine Mixtures)

**Source:** 中国出口管制信息网
**URL:** http://exportcontrol.mofcom.gov.cn/article/zcfg/gnzcfg/zcfggzqd/202402/961.html
**Date:** 2024-02-23
**Category:** regulation_update
**Fetched:** 2026-01-28
**Confidence:** High

---

## Summary

工业和信息化部、商务部、海关总署联合发布通知,优化对低浓度三乙醇胺混合物的进出口监管措施。自2024年2月1日起,符合条件的非医用消毒剂、合成洗涤粉、化妆品、墨水等消费类商品免于办理监控化学品进出口审批和两用物项出口许可手续。

---

## Key Details

**Issuing Authority:**
- 工业和信息化部办公厅 (General Office of MIIT)
- 商务部办公厅 (General Office of MOFCOM)
- 海关总署办公厅 (General Office of GAC)

**Effective Date:** 2024-02-01

**Scope:**
- 三乙醇胺含量较低的非医用消毒剂 (Low-concentration non-medical disinfectants containing triethanolamine)
- 合成洗涤粉 (Synthetic detergent powder)
- 化妆品 (Cosmetics)
- 墨水等消费类商品 (Ink and other consumer products)

---

## Key Provisions

1. **Exemption from Controlled Chemical Permits:** 符合条件的产品无需办理监控化学品进出口审批手续 (Eligible products are exempt from controlled chemical import-export approval procedures)

2. **Exemption from Dual-Use License:** 无需办理两用物项和技术进出口许可 (No dual-use item and technology import-export license required)

3. **Product List:** 通知附有"不属于《两用物项和技术进出口许可证管理目录》的低浓度三乙醇胺混合物产品清单" (Notice includes a list of low-concentration triethanolamine mixture products not subject to the Dual-Use License Management Catalog)

4. **Publication Date:** 通知发布日期为2024年1月16日 (Notice published on January 16, 2024)

---

## Supply Chain Impact Assessment

**Direct Impact:** 该措施显著简化了含低浓度三乙醇胺消费品的贸易流程,预期将加快化妆品、清洁用品、办公用品等日用消费品的通关效率 (This measure significantly streamlines trade procedures for consumer products containing low-concentration triethanolamine, expected to accelerate customs clearance efficiency for daily consumer goods including cosmetics, cleaning supplies, and office products)

**Administrative Burden Reduction:** 企业无需申请双重许可证,可降低合规成本和时间 (Companies no longer need to apply for dual licenses, reducing compliance costs and time) *(speculative)*

**Sectoral Beneficiaries:** 化妆品制造商、日化产品出口商、印刷耗材供应商预期受益 (Cosmetics manufacturers, daily chemical product exporters, and printing consumables suppliers expected to benefit) *(speculative)*

---

## Notes

此通知体现了中国出口管制政策的精准化趋势,针对低风险民用消费品实施监管优化,同时保持对高风险化学品的严格管控。企业应参照附件产品清单确认适用范围。
