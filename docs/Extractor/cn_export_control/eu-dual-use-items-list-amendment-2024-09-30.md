## Summary
The EU Commission adopted a delegated regulation on September 5, 2024, modifying the EU dual-use items export control list in Annex I of Regulation (EU) 2021/821. The revision aligns the EU list with the latest control lists of four major international export control multilateral regimes.

## Key Details
- **Issuing Authority**: European Commission (EU Commission)
- **Effective Date**: Not specified (delegated regulation adoption date: 2024-09-05)
- **Scope**: Annex I of Regulation (EU) 2021/821 on export of dual-use items

## Key Provisions
- **Legal Framework**: Modifies Annex I of Regulation (EU) 2021/821, the EU's comprehensive dual-use export control regulation
- **Harmonization Objective**: Aligns EU controls with latest lists from four major multilateral export control regimes:
  - Nuclear Suppliers Group (NSG)
  - Australia Group (AG)
  - Missile Technology Control Regime (MTCR)
  - Wassenaar Arrangement (WA)
- **Delegated Regulation**: Allows Commission to update technical annexes without full legislative process
- **Systematic Update**: Ensures EU dual-use controls remain synchronized with international standards
