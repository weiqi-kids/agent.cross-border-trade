## Summary

The UK Export Control Joint Unit (ECJU) released the "2024 UK Strategic Export Controls Annual Report" (《2024年英国战略出口管制年度报告》) on July 18, 2025. The report documents 2024 UK strategic export control policy and licensing activity across five licence categories, reporting application, issuance, and refusal statistics.

## Key Details

- **Issuing Authority**: UK Export Control Joint Unit (ECJU)
- **Effective Date**: Report published 2025-07-18 (covers calendar year 2024)
- **Scope**: UK strategic export control licensing across five categories

## Key Provisions

2024 licensing statistics by category:

- **Standard Individual Export Licences (SIELs)**: 15,253 applications, 10,814 issued, 600 denied.
- **Open Individual Export Licences (OIELs)**: 375 applications, 316 issued, 62 denied.
- **Standard Individual Transhipment Licences (SITLs)**: 12 applications, 1 issued.
- **Standard Individual Trade Control Licences (SITCLs)**: 206 applications, 116 issued, 11 denied.
- **Open Individual Trade Control Licences (OITCLs)**: 34 applications, 15 issued, 12 denied.

Report outlines 2024 UK strategic export control policies and licence issuance practices across these regulatory categories.
