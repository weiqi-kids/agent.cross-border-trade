## Summary

On May 25-26, 2026, the third meeting of the China-Republic of Korea (ROK) Export Control Dialogue Mechanism was held in Xi'an, Shaanxi Province, alongside a government-business exchange forum. The two sides agreed to further strengthen dialogue and cooperation in the export control field and to promote compliant trade of dual-use items (两用物项 / dual-use items) between China and the ROK.

- **Issuing Authority**: China Ministry of Commerce (商务部), Security and Control Bureau (安全与管制局)

## Key Provisions

- **Participants**: China — Jiang Qianliang (江前良), Director of the Security and Control Bureau, MOFCOM; ROK — Kim Taeyou, Director of the Trade Security Policy Bureau.
- **Outcome**: The two sides agreed to "进一步加强出口管制领域对话合作，促进和发展中韩两用物项合规贸易" (further strengthen dialogue and cooperation in the export control field and promote/develop compliant dual-use items trade between China and the ROK).
- **Side event**: Both governments jointly hosted a business-government (政企) exchange forum for industry representatives from the two countries.
