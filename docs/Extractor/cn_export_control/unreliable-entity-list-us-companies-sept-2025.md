# 将萨罗尼克科技公司等3家美国企业列入不可靠实体清单 (Three US Entities Added to Unreliable Entity List)

**Source:** 商务部 (Ministry of Commerce)
**URL:** http://exportcontrol.mofcom.gov.cn/article/zcfg/gnzcfg/zcfggzqd/202509/1165.html
**Date:** 2025-09-26
**Category:** `enforcement_action`
**Fetched:** 2026-01-28
**Confidence:** High

---

## Summary

The Unreliable Entity List Working Mechanism issued Announcement No. 9 of 2025, adding three US companies to China's Unreliable Entity List effective September 25, 2025. The designated entities face comprehensive prohibitions on conducting import/export activities with China and making new investments within Chinese territory.

---

## Key Details

**Issuing Authority:** Unreliable Entity List Working Mechanism (不可靠实体清单工作机制)

**Effective Date:** 2025-09-25 (upon publication)

**Scope:** Three US entities:
- Saronic Technologies, Inc. (萨罗尼克科技公司)
- Aerkomm Inc. (艾科姆公司)
- Oceaneering International, Inc. (海洋工程国际公司)

---

## Key Provisions

1. **Legal Basis:** Foreign Trade Law, National Security Law, and Unreliable Entity List Regulations (Articles 2, 8, and 10)

2. **Import/Export Prohibition:** "禁止上述企业从事与中国有关的进出口活动" — Listed entities are prohibited from conducting import/export activities related to China

3. **Investment Prohibition:** "禁止上述企业在中国境内新增投资" — Listed entities are prohibited from making new investments within China

4. **Enforcement Mechanism:** Violations subject to penalties under the Unreliable Entity List Regulations

---

## Supply Chain Impact Assessment

**Impact Level:** High

**Affected Industries:**
- Maritime technology and offshore services (Oceaneering International)
- Autonomous maritime systems (Saronic Technologies)
- Aviation connectivity and telecommunications (Aerkomm)

**Geographic Considerations (speculative):**
- Chinese entities conducting business with these companies must immediately cease transactions
- Existing contracts and ongoing projects likely face termination or restructuring
- Alternative suppliers for marine engineering and offshore services may see increased demand from Chinese clients
- Global supply chains involving these entities and Chinese counterparts will require reconfiguration

**Compliance Requirements:**
- Chinese companies must conduct due diligence to identify existing business relationships with listed entities
- Immediate cessation of import/export activities required
- No new investment transactions permitted

---

## Notes

This enforcement action represents China's use of the Unreliable Entity List as a countermeasure tool. The timing and selection of entities suggest coordination with broader geopolitical considerations. Companies should monitor for potential expansion of the list and establish compliance protocols for entity screening.
