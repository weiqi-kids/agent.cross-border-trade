# 含γ-丁内酯部分特定产品优化服务管理措施的公告 (Announcement on Optimizing Service Management Measures for Certain Products Containing γ-Butyrolactone)

**Source:** 商务部网站
**URL:** http://exportcontrol.mofcom.gov.cn/article/zcfg/gnzcfg/zcfggzqd/202401/947.html
**Date:** 2024-01-03
**Category:** regulation_update
**Fetched:** 2026-01-28
**Confidence:** High

---

## Summary

公安部、商务部、国家卫生健康委、应急管理部、海关总署、国家药品监管局六部门联合发布公告,对含γ-丁内酯(GBL)的光刻胶、打印墨水等特定产品实施优化管理。符合浓度标准的产品免于办理进出口许可和国内购买运输备案,自2024年1月1日起施行。

---

## Key Details

**Issuing Authority:**
- 公安部 (Ministry of Public Security)
- 商务部 (Ministry of Commerce)
- 国家卫生健康委 (National Health Commission)
- 应急管理部 (Ministry of Emergency Management)
- 海关总署 (General Administration of Customs)
- 国家药品监管局 (National Medical Products Administration)

**Effective Date:** 2024-01-01

**Scope:**
- 光刻胶 (Photoresist)
- 聚酰亚胺取向液 (Polyimide alignment agent)
- 稀释剂 (Diluent)
- 打印墨水及耗材 (Printing ink and consumables)

---

## Key Provisions

1. **Photoresist Products (GBL Content < 60%):**
   - 免于办理进口许可 (Exempt from import license)
   - 免于办理国内购买和运输备案证明 (Exempt from domestic purchase and transportation filing)

2. **Printing Ink Products (GBL Content < 20%):**
   - 免于办理进出口许可 (Exempt from import-export license)
   - 免于办理国内经营、购买和运输备案证明 (Exempt from domestic operation, purchase, and transportation filing)

3. **Enterprise Compliance Requirements:**
   - 建立内部安全管理机制 (Establish internal safety management mechanisms)
   - 如实申报易制毒化学品含量 (Truthfully declare precursor chemical content)
   - 建立相关台账 (Maintain relevant records)

4. **Legal Basis:** 基于易制毒化学品管理条例实施 (Implemented based on Precursor Chemical Management Regulations)

---

## Supply Chain Impact Assessment

**Semiconductor Industry:** 该措施显著降低了半导体制造企业进口光刻胶的行政负担,预期将加快芯片制造关键材料的供应链流通速度 (This measure significantly reduces administrative burden for semiconductor manufacturers importing photoresist, expected to accelerate supply chain flow of critical chip manufacturing materials) *(speculative)*

**Printing Industry:** 打印耗材企业的跨境贸易流程简化,有利于降低物流成本和缩短交货周期 (Streamlined cross-border trade procedures for printing consumables enterprises, conducive to reducing logistics costs and shortening delivery cycles) *(speculative)*

**Compliance Management:** 企业需建立完善的内控机制,虽免除许可但需保持记录可追溯性 (Enterprises need to establish comprehensive internal controls; while licenses are exempted, record traceability must be maintained) *(speculative)*

## Notes

此公告平衡了反毒品管制与产业发展需求,对半导体、印刷等行业具有积极意义。企业应注意浓度阈值的严格界定,并履行如实申报义务以避免违规风险。GBL作为第一类易制毒化学品,其管理优化需要多部门协同,体现了中国出口管制政策的精细化导向。
