# 中华人民共和国商务部令二〇二六年第1号 关于取消对欧盟两家金融机构反制措施的决定（MOFCOM Order 2026 No. 1: Decision to Cancel Countermeasures Against Two EU Financial Institutions）

## Summary

MOFCOM Order 2026 No. 1 cancels the countermeasures (反制措施) previously imposed by MOFCOM Order 2025 No. 5 on two EU financial institutions and removes them from the countermeasure list (反制清单). The decision was made in response to the EU's announcement on 2026-04-23 to revoke sanctions against two Chinese financial institutions. The action is grounded in the Anti-Foreign Sanctions Law of the PRC and its implementing provisions.

## Key Details

- **Issuing Authority**: Ministry of Commerce of the PRC (中华人民共和国商务部)
- **Effective Date**: 2026-04-24 (自北京时间 2026年4月24日起 / from Beijing time)
- **Scope**: Two EU financial institutions previously subject to PRC countermeasures

## Key Provisions

- **Institutions de-listed (2)**:
  1. UAB Urbo Bankas
  2. AB Mano Bankas
- **Core decision**: Effective from Beijing time 2026-04-24, the countermeasures imposed under MOFCOM Order 2025 No. 5 (商务部令二〇二五年第5号) on the two banks are cancelled, and the banks are removed from the countermeasure list (反制清单).
- **Trigger/reason**: 「鉴于2026年4月23日欧盟宣布撤销对中国两家金融机构的制裁」— in view of the EU's 2026-04-23 announcement revoking sanctions against two Chinese financial institutions, China made a reciprocal decision.
- **Legal basis**: 《中华人民共和国反外国制裁法》(Anti-Foreign Sanctions Law of the PRC) and 《实施〈中华人民共和国反外国制裁法〉的规定》(Provisions on Implementing the Anti-Foreign Sanctions Law of the PRC).

## Supply Chain Impact Assessment
