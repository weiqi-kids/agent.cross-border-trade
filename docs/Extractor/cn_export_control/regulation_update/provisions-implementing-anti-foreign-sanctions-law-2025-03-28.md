## Summary

The 实施《中华人民共和国反外国制裁法》的规定 (Provisions on Implementing the Anti-Foreign Sanctions Law of the PRC) is an implementing regulation comprising 22 articles, formulated under the Foreign Relations Law and the Anti-Foreign Sanctions Law of the PRC. It establishes the framework for placing foreign states, organizations, or individuals on the countermeasure list, the specific countermeasures available, the relief/exemption application mechanism, and penalties for complying with foreign discriminatory restrictive measures. It takes effect upon publication.

## Key Details

- **Issuing Authority**: Ministry of Commerce of the PRC (中华人民共和国商务部)
- **Effective Date**: Upon publication (自公布之日起施行)
- **Scope**: Foreign states, organizations, and individuals engaged in containment, suppression, or interference in China's internal affairs; entities/persons subject to countermeasures; total of 22 articles

## Key Provisions

- **Total articles**: 22 articles (共22条).
- **Scope of countermeasures (Art. 3)**: Where foreign states, organizations, or individuals contain/suppress China or interfere in its internal affairs, relevant State Council departments may place them on the countermeasure list (反制清单).
- **Specific countermeasures (Arts. 6–9)**:
  - 「不予签发签证、不准入境、注销签证或驱逐出境」— denial/cancellation of visas, denial of entry, or deportation.
  - 「查封、扣押、冻结」— sealing, seizure, and freezing of property, intellectual property, etc.
  - Prohibition or restriction of transactions and cooperation in relevant fields.
  - Prohibition of import/export activities, restriction of investment, and imposition of fines.
- **Relief mechanism (Arts. 14–16)**: Organizations or individuals subject to countermeasures may apply to suspend, modify, or cancel the measures, or apply for exemption in special circumstances.
- **Penalties for non-compliance (Art. 17)**: Those who implement foreign discriminatory restrictive measures may be summoned for talks (约谈), ordered to rectify, and otherwise dealt with.
- **Legal basis**: 《中华人民共和国对外关系法》(Foreign Relations Law of the PRC) and 《中华人民共和国反外国制裁法》(Anti-Foreign Sanctions Law of the PRC).

## Supply Chain Impact Assessment

*Speculative analysis.* These implementing provisions operationalize China's countermeasure regime, increasing compliance complexity for multinationals that must reconcile foreign sanctions obligations with the risk of Chinese countermeasures (a "compliance trap"). Companies implementing third-country discriminatory measures against Chinese entities face possible asset freezes, transaction bans, and import/export prohibitions in China. The relief/exemption mechanism offers a procedural off-ramp but adds administrative burden. Heightens dual-compliance risk for finance, logistics, and trading firms operating across both jurisdictions.
