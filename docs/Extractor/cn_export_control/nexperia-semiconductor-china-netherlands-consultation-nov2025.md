## Summary

商务部新闻发言人回应中荷双方就安世半导体(Nexperia)问题的磋商情况。中方强调造成全球半导体供应链混乱的源头和责任在荷方,欢迎荷方于11月18-19日在北京进行的两轮面对面磋商并暂停行政令,但认为这仅是第一步,距离完全解决问题还需撤销该令。中方指出荷兰企业法庭剥夺闻泰科技控制权的裁决是关键阻碍,双方同意取消行政干预,支持企业通过协商依法解决纠纷。

## Key Provisions

1. **Consultation Timeline:** 中荷双方于2025年11月18-19日在北京进行了两轮面对面磋商 (China-Netherlands held two rounds of face-to-face consultations in Beijing on November 18-19, 2025)

2. **China's Position on Responsibility:** "造成当前全球半导体产供链混乱的源头和责任在荷方" (The source and responsibility for current global semiconductor supply chain disruption lies with the Netherlands)

3. **Administrative Order Status:** 欢迎荷方暂停行政令,但认为这仅是"第一步",距离完全解决问题还需撤销该令 (Welcome Netherlands' suspension of administrative order, but view this as only "the first step"; full resolution requires its revocation)

4. **Enterprise Dispute Resolution:** 荷兰企业法庭剥夺闻泰科技(Wingtech)控制权的裁决是"关键阻碍",双方同意取消行政干预,支持企业通过协商依法解决纠纷 (Netherlands Enterprise Court ruling stripping Wingtech of control is "key obstacle"; both sides agree to cancel administrative intervention and support enterprises resolving disputes through negotiation and legal channels)

5. **Policy Objective:** 旨在恢复全球半导体供应链的安全与稳定 (Aims to restore security and stability of global semiconductor supply chain)

---

## Supply Chain Impact Assessment

**Semiconductor Supply Chain Vulnerability:** 安世半导体(Nexperia)是全球重要的功率半导体和逻辑芯片供应商,中荷争端直接影响汽车电子、工业控制、消费电子等下游产业的元器件供应稳定性 (Nexperia is a major global supplier of power semiconductors and logic chips; China-Netherlands dispute directly impacts component supply stability for downstream industries including automotive electronics, industrial controls, and consumer electronics) *(speculative)*

**Administrative Intervention Precedent:** 荷兰援引《货物可用性法案》干预企业股权控制,可能引发其他国家仿效,增加跨国并购和供应链整合的政治风险 (Netherlands invoking Goods Availability Act to intervene in corporate equity control may trigger emulation by other countries, increasing political risks in cross-border M&A and supply chain integration) *(speculative)*

**China-Europe Tech Trade Relations:** 该争端反映出中欧在半导体领域的战略分歧,可能影响未来中欧科技产业合作和供应链互信 (This dispute reflects China-EU strategic divergence in semiconductor sector, may impact future China-EU tech industry cooperation and supply chain trust) *(speculative)*

**Resolution Pathway:** 双方同意通过协商解决,表明中荷都希望避免供应链完全脱钩,但进展取决于荷方是否撤销行政令和法院裁决 (Both sides agree to resolve through consultation, indicating both China and Netherlands wish to avoid complete supply chain decoupling, but progress depends on whether Netherlands revokes administrative order and court ruling) *(speculative)*

---

## Notes

安世半导体争端源于2019年中国闻泰科技收购荷兰安世半导体(原NXP子公司),2023年荷兰政府援引冷战时期制定的《货物可用性法案》介入并通过企业法庭剥夺闻泰科技的控制权,引发中方强烈反应。中方将此视为行政干预企业自主经营的典型案例,并将全球半导体供应链混乱责任归咎于荷方。此次磋商表明双方正在寻求外交解决方案,但中方坚持要求荷方撤销行政令而非仅暂停,显示立场仍有分歧。对于跨国科技企业和供应链管理者,此案例凸显了地缘政治风险对并购整合和供应链控制权的现实影响。
