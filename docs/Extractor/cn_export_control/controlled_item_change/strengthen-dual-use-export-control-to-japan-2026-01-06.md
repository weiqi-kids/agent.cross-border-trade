# 商务部公告2026年第1号 关于加强两用物项对日本出口管制的公告（MOFCOM Announcement 2026 No. 1: Strengthening Dual-Use Item Export Controls to Japan）

## Summary

MOFCOM Announcement 2026 No. 1 strengthens export controls on dual-use items (两用物项) destined for Japan. The core measure prohibits the export of all dual-use items to Japanese military end-users, for military purposes, and for any other end-use or end-user that helps enhance Japan's military capabilities. The measure is grounded in the Export Control Law of the PRC and is justified on national-security and international non-proliferation grounds. Violators in any country who transfer or supply PRC-origin dual-use items to such Japanese end-uses face legal liability.

## Key Details

- **Issuing Authority**: Ministry of Commerce of the PRC (中华人民共和国商务部)
- **Effective Date**: 2026-01-06 (自公布之日起施行 / from date of publication)
- **Scope**: All dual-use items destined for Japan where the end-user/end-use is military or contributes to Japanese military capability; applies extraterritorially to re-transfer of PRC-origin dual-use items by any country's entities

## Key Provisions

- **Core prohibition**: 「禁止所有两用物项对日本军事用户、军事用途，以及一切有助于提升日本军事实力的其他最终用户用途出口」— prohibition on exporting all dual-use items (dual-use items = 两用物项) to Japanese military end-users, for military purposes, and for any other end-user/end-use that helps enhance Japan's military strength.
- **Extraterritorial reach**: Any organization or individual from any country that violates these provisions by transferring or providing PRC-origin dual-use items to the prohibited Japanese end-uses bears legal liability.
- **Justification**: Protection of national security and fulfillment of international non-proliferation obligations.
- **Legal basis**: 《中华人民共和国出口管制法》(Export Control Law of the PRC) and related regulations.
- **Item-level detail**: The specific affected item list is not enumerated in this announcement; it references the dual-use items export control system/database maintained by the ministry.

## Supply Chain Impact Assessment

*Speculative analysis.* A country-targeted, military-end-use prohibition on dual-use exports to Japan is a significant escalation in China-Japan trade-control relations. Exporters and their global affiliates must implement end-user/end-use screening for Japan-bound dual-use shipments and re-exports of PRC-origin goods, raising compliance costs and creating transaction risk for defense-adjacent supply chains. Japanese firms in semiconductors, advanced materials, and machinery that source PRC-origin dual-use inputs may face supply disruption where end-use cannot be cleared. The extraterritorial liability clause extends compliance exposure to third-country distributors.
