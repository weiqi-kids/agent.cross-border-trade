## Summary

The Ministry of Commerce (MOFCOM) and Ministry of Science and Technology (MOST) jointly issued Announcement 2025 No. 28, making partial adjustments (部分调整) to the 《中国禁止出口限制出口技术目录》 (China Catalogue of Technologies Prohibited or Restricted from Export), which was previously released under Announcement 2023 No. 57. The adjustment is made pursuant to the Foreign Trade Law and the Regulations on Technology Import-Export Management of the PRC. The specific list of added, removed, or reclassified technology items is contained in a referenced PDF attachment not fully reproduced on the article page.

## Key Details

- **Issuing Authority**: Ministry of Commerce (商务部) and Ministry of Science and Technology (科技部)
- **Effective Date**: Upon publication (自公布之日起施行); article publish date 2025-07-15
- **Scope**: National catalogue of export-prohibited and export-restricted technologies; dual-use (military-civilian) technologies to be brought under export control management

## Key Provisions

- **Action**: Partial adjustment (部分调整) and re-release of the 《中国禁止出口限制出口技术目录》 (Catalogue of Technologies Prohibited or Restricted from Export), superseding the prior version in Announcement 2023 No. 57.
- **Dual-use note**: Technologies with both military and civilian applications are to be included under export control management.
- **Legal basis**: 《中华人民共和国对外贸易法》(Foreign Trade Law of the PRC) and 《中华人民共和国技术进出口管理条例》(Regulations on Technology Import-Export Management of the PRC).
- **Detailed item list**: The specific prohibited / restricted technology entries (additions, deletions, reclassifications) are provided in an attached PDF referenced by the announcement; the item-level changes are not reproduced in the fetched article body.
