# 商务部公告2026年第20号 公布将7家欧盟实体列入出口管制管控名单（MOFCOM Announcement 2026 No. 20: Adding 7 EU Entities to the Export Control List）

## Summary

China's Ministry of Commerce (MOFCOM) Announcement 2026 No. 20 places 7 European Union entities on the export control list, prohibiting Chinese exporters from supplying dual-use items (两用物项) to these entities. Ongoing export activities to the listed entities must cease immediately, with limited exceptions available only upon special application to MOFCOM. The measure is grounded in the Export Control Law and the Regulations on Export Control of Dual-Use Items of the PRC.

## Key Details

- **Issuing Authority**: Ministry of Commerce of the PRC, Security and Control Bureau (中华人民共和国商务部安全与管制局)
- **Effective Date**: 2026-04-24 (生效日期 2026年4月24日)
- **Scope**: 7 named EU entities (Belgium, Czech Republic, Germany); all dual-use items destined for these entities; primarily defense/aerospace-related companies

## Key Provisions

- **Listed entities (7)** — Chinese exporters prohibited from exporting dual-use items to:
  1. FN Herstal (Belgium)
  2. OMNIPOL a.s. (Czech Republic)
  3. HENSOLDT AG (Germany)
  4. EXCALIBUR ARMY spol. s.r.o. (Czech Republic)
  5. SPACEKNOW INC., Czech branch
  6. VZLU AEROSPACE a.s. (Czech Republic)
  7. FN Browning (Belgium)
- **Core prohibition**: 「禁止出口经营者向上述7家实体出口两用物项」— exporters are prohibited from exporting dual-use items (dual-use items = 两用物项) to the 7 listed entities.
- **Cessation of ongoing activity**: In-progress export activities to the listed entities must be halted immediately.
- **Exception mechanism**: In special circumstances, exports may be authorized upon application to and approval by MOFCOM.
- **Legal basis**: 《中华人民共和国出口管制法》(Export Control Law of the PRC) and 《中华人民共和国两用物项出口管制条例》(Regulations on Export Control of Dual-Use Items of the PRC).
