# 关于将4-哌啶酮和1-叔丁氧羰基-4-哌啶酮列为易制毒化学品管理的公告（Announcement on Listing 4-Piperidone and 1-Boc-4-Piperidone as Controlled Precursor Chemicals）

## Summary

Six Chinese government departments jointly issued an announcement listing 4-哌啶酮 (4-piperidone) and 1-叔丁氧羰基-4-哌啶酮 (1-Boc-4-piperidone) as controlled precursor chemicals (易制毒化学品 / precursor chemicals) under the Precursor Chemical Management Regulations. The two chemicals are added to the precursor-chemical classification and product directory and become subject to precursor-chemical management requirements.

## Key Details

- **Issuing Authority**: Six departments jointly — Ministry of Public Security (公安部), Ministry of Commerce (商务部), National Health Commission (国家卫生健康委), Ministry of Emergency Management (应急管理部), General Administration of Customs (海关总署), and National Medical Products Administration (国家药监局)
- **Effective Date**: 2025-07-20 (自2025年7月20日起施行)
- **Scope**: Two specified chemicals used as fentanyl-class precursors; affects production, operation, purchase, transport, import and export of the listed substances

## Key Provisions

- **Legal basis**: 《易制毒化学品管理条例》(Regulations on the Administration of Precursor Chemicals).
- **Newly controlled chemicals**:
  1. 4-哌啶酮 (4-piperidone)
  2. 1-叔丁氧羰基-4-哌啶酮 (1-Boc-4-piperidone / tert-butyl 4-oxopiperidine-1-carboxylate)
- **Control measure**: Both chemicals are listed in the precursor-chemical classification and product directory and managed as controlled precursor chemicals, subjecting them to licensing/filing, and import-export controls under the regulations.

## Supply Chain Impact Assessment

*The following is speculative analysis.* As precursors associated with fentanyl-class synthesis, the listing imposes import/export licensing and record-keeping obligations on chemical manufacturers, traders and logistics providers handling these substances. Exporters of these chemicals from China will face new permit requirements, and the move aligns with international counter-narcotics cooperation pressure. Pharmaceutical and fine-chemical supply chains using 4-piperidone intermediates should anticipate added compliance steps from the effective date.
