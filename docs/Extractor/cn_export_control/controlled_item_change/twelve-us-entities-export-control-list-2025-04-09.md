# 商务部公告2025年第22号 公布将12家美国实体列入出口管制管控名单（MOFCOM Announcement No.22 of 2025 — Adding Twelve US Entities to the Export Control List）

## Summary

China's Ministry of Commerce issued Announcement No.22 of 2025, adding twelve US entities to the export control list (出口管制管控名单). Exporting dual-use items to these twelve entities is prohibited, ongoing related export activities must cease immediately, and any exceptional exports require MOFCOM approval. The measures took effect at 00:01 on April 10, 2025, citing national security and non-proliferation obligations.

## Key Details

- **Issuing Authority**: Security and Control Bureau, Ministry of Commerce (商务部安全与管制局)
- **Effective Date**: 2025-04-10, 00:01
- **Scope**: Twelve US defense, photonics, drone and aerospace entities; controls apply to dual-use items (两用物项 / dual-use items)

## Key Provisions

- **Legal basis**: 《中华人民共和国出口管制法》(Export Control Law of the PRC) and 《中华人民共和国两用物项出口管制条例》(Regulations on Export Control of Dual-Use Items of the PRC).
- **Twelve listed US entities**:
  1. American Photonics
  2. Novotech, Inc.
  3. Echodyne
  4. Marvin Engineering Company, Inc.
  5. Exovera
  6. Teledyne Brown Engineering, Inc.
  7. BRINC Drones, Inc.
  8. SYNEXXUS, Inc.
  9. Firestorm Labs, Inc.
  10. Kratos Unmanned Aerial Systems, Inc.
  11. Domo Tactical Communications
  12. Insitu, Inc.
- **Control measures**:
  - Prohibition on exporting dual-use items to the twelve listed US entities.
  - Ongoing related export activities must cease immediately.
  - Exports in special circumstances require approval from the Ministry of Commerce.

## Supply Chain Impact Assessment

*The following is speculative analysis.* The listing blocks the twelve US entities — concentrated in defense electronics, photonics and unmanned systems — from receiving Chinese-origin dual-use items, components and materials. Issued amid the April 2025 US-China trade escalation, it pressures these firms' access to Chinese inputs such as rare-earth-derived materials and electronic components. Exporters with China nexus must screen against the updated control list before fulfilling orders to these entities.
